# 04-concepts

Atomic, Obsidian-linked notes — one idea each, 2–4 sentences, heavily linked rather than heavily detailed (e.g. `btool`, `bucket-states`, `fishbucket`). They grow organically as cross-references between topic syntheses and labs, and they make the Obsidian graph view useful. The goal is connection density, not depth: depth belongs in `03-topics/`, the atom just names the thing and points to where it matters.
